The Best Anime According To Gaurav

My top 3 anime of all time

Naruto

Naruto follows the story of Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja who dreams of becoming the strongest ninja and earning the respect of his village. Born with a powerful beast sealed inside him, Naruto grows up facing loneliness and rejection, but never gives up on his goal. The anime focuses on friendship, hard work, perseverance, and personal growth, showing how determination can overcome natural talent.

Attack on Titan

Attack on Titan is set in a world where humanity lives behind massive walls to protect itself from giant humanoid creatures called Titans. The story follows Eren Yeager and his friends as they join the military to fight Titans and uncover the truth about their world. The anime is known for its intense action, dark themes, political conflicts, and shocking plot twists.

Death Note

Death Note centers around Light Yagami, a highly intelligent student who finds a mysterious notebook that can kill anyone whose name is written in it. Believing he can create a perfect world, Light begins eliminating criminals, attracting the attention of a genius detective known as L. The anime is a psychological thriller that explores morality, justice, power, and the consequences of playing god.
